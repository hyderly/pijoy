PI. Joy – Corporate Affiliation

PI. Joy's Corporate Affiliations

As a leading serial entrepreneur, P.I. Joy holds many important positions in his own companies. He has worked hard to develop different enterprises and tapped various industries. He is a successful businessman who is a leader by heart and knows how to instill motivation among his employees. Let’s have a brief look at his corporate affiliations.

Financial Sector

Former Board Chairman of American United Bank

Brief Bank’s Profile

American United Bank was a commercial bank that provided a complete range of banking services to the public. It focused on building business relationships in the Gwinnett and surrounding metro Atlanta counties. It provided a variety of credit facilities, deposit products, and other financial services to facilitate both business and consumer banking relationships.

Under PI. Joy's leadership, AUB raised 10.1 Million dollars in capital within six weeks' time, which broke all records for raising capital. They had eleven board of directors, including prominent businessmen, professionals, community leaders, and well-known diplomats. It represented diversity as the members of the board are from diverse ethnic backgrounds, including Americans, Asian-Indians, and Jewish communities
